About the Firm

Built on Client Relationships, Not Volume

Since 2009, Mathews CPA Inc. has served individuals, families, and small businesses across Austin and Central Texas. What started as a commitment to personalized accounting has grown into a practice that clients across the country — and internationally — rely on.

We specialize in working with clients who need more than a tax return. Whether it's navigating IRS disputes, managing foreign account compliance, or building a financial strategy for a growing business, we bring hands-on attention that larger firms can't match.

With offices in Austin and Cedar Park, and clients across the U.S. and Canada, Mathews CPA combines local roots with the expertise to handle complex, multi-jurisdictional financial needs.
