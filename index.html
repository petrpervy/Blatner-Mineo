About the Firm

Columbus's Go-To Tax Team

George Gantner Taxes is a full-service tax, accounting, and business consulting firm in the heart of Columbus's German Village. For over a decade, we've helped individuals and businesses navigate their finances with a simple philosophy: be affordable, be thorough, and be available.

Our clients don't come to us because we're the biggest firm in town. They come because we pick up the phone, we explain things in plain English, and we charge fair prices. Whether you're filing a straightforward personal return or dealing with a complicated IRS situation, we've seen it and we've fixed it.

We're open 7 days a week during tax season because we know your schedule doesn't stop on Friday. That's the kind of firm we are.
